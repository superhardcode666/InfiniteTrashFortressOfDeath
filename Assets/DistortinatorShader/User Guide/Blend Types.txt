Most common Blend Types:

Traditional transparency:
- Source: SrcAlpha
- Destination: OneMinusSrcAlpha 

Premultiplied transparency:
- Source: One
- Destination: OneMinusSrcAlpha 

Additive:
- Source: One
- Destination: One 

Soft Additive:
- Source: OneMinusDstColor
- Destination: One 

Multiplicative:
- Source: DstColor
- Destination: Zero 

2x Multiplicative:
- Source: DstColor
- Destination: SrcColor
